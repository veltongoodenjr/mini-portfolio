Privacy Notice

This page explains how your information is handled when you contact me.

What I collect

Your name, email, and message. Phone and links are optional.

Why I collect it

To read and respond to your inquiry and plan next steps for a potential project.

How it’s processed

Your message is sent to my business email. I don’t sell or share your contact details with third parties for marketing.

Messages are stored in email. I aim to keep inquiries no longer than 12 months unless needed for an active engagement.

Your choices

You can request a copy or deletion of your message by emailing info@veltongoodenjr.com.

Processors & hosting

Email is handled via Google Workspace. Website is hosted on GitHub Pages.
